KYLIE COSMETICS

Kylie Cosmetics, LLC is an American cosmetics company founded by Kylie Jenner. The company began selling Kylie Lip Kits, a liquid lipstick and lip liner set, on November 30, 2015. Formerly known as Kylie Lip Kits, the company was renamed Kylie Cosmetics.

New updated line features all of my favorite iconic Lip Kit shades as well as my must-haves including blushes, bronzers, highlighters, high glosses, matte liquid lipsticks, lip blushes, brow essentials and eyeliners. Everything is clean, vegan, with ingredients that are good for your skin, but I made sure we didn’t sacrifice quality, pigmentation or performance. For me, being able to have a lasting matte lip formula that wasn’t overly drying, was so important when reformulating my Lip Kit. My new formula has 8 hour wear and lasting color but is super comfortable on the lips.

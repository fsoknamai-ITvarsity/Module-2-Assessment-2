NOPE

Nope is a 2022 American science fiction horror film written, directed, and co-produced by Jordan Peele under his Monkeypaw Productions banner. Wikipedia Release date: 22 July 2022 (USA) Director: Jordan Peele Distributed by: Universal Pictures

The official MMPA Nope age rating is R. This means it's not suitable for children under the age of 18.

CAST

Keke Palmer as Emarald Hay, Daniel Kaluuya as OJ, Brandon Perea as Angel Torres, Barbie Ferreira as Nessie and Micheal Wincott as Antlers Holst.

BEAST

Recently widowed Dr. Nate Daniels and his two teenage daughters travel to a South African game reserve managed by Martin Battles, an old family friend and wildlife biologist. However, what begins as a journey of healing soon turns into a fearsome fight for survival when a lion, a survivor of bloodthirsty poachers, begins stalking them. Release date: 19 August 2022 (USA) Director: Baltasar Kormákur Distributed by: Universal Pictures

Beast (2022) is rated R for blood, violent content and language.

MR PRICE

Mr Price Group Limited, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa. Founded in 1985, Mr Price opened in 1987 after the owners bought a controlling interest in John Orrs Holdings, whose trading divisions at the time were The Hub and Miladys.

Mr Price group is a retailer active in the fashion and clothing segment of the market. The retail in apparel, homeware and sportswear and is one of the fastest growing retailers in South Africa. Mr Price's cash sales constitute 82.6% of total sales and the group is focused on remaining a cash-driven retailer.

BULLET TRAIN

Bullet Train is a 2022 American action comedy film directed by David Leitch from a screenplay by Zak Olkewicz. It is based on the 2010 novel Maria Beetle by Kōtarō Isaka. The film stars Brad Pitt as a former assassin who must battle fellow killers while riding a Tokaido Shinkansen Hikari train.

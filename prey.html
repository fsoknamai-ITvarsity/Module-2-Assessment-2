PREY

Prey is a 2022 American science fiction action horror film based on the Predator franchise. It is the fifth installment in the franchise and is a prequel to the first four films, being set in the Northern Great Plains in North America in 1719. The film is directed by Dan Trachtenberg and written by Patrick Aison. Wikipedia Release date: 21 July 2022 (USA) Director: Dan Trachtenberg Distributed by: Disney+, Hulu

Prey is Rated R for strong bloody violence.

CAST

Amber Midthunder as Naru, Dane DiLiegro as Predator, Dakota Beevers as Taabe, Stefani Mathias and Michelle Trush as Aruka.
